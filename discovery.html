Discovery Log

Security Management Principles:

Week 2 — Web Security

In this first week starting my university course, we discussed security principles for using the web. We talked about the CIA (confidentiality, integrity, and availability) of information, and the potential implications a business could have if their security management principles were to fail.

Reviewing Literature:

Week 3 — Reviewing Literature

In this week we covered how to review literature as we were handed our first literature review coursework to complete. The lecture covered basic rules to ensure we used literature effectively within our review, and how to avoid using sources that are not published literature.

Academic Writing:

Week 4 — Academic Writing

In this week we looked at writing principles to ensure we have the right skills and writing techniques to create an outstanding literature review. We looked at structure, form, and grammar. We also looked at key natural writing principles to avoid.

Introduction to Markup:

Week 5 — Introduction to Markup

In this week, we covered the basics of markup. We discussed its use and were asked questions regarding using markup languages before. We looked at HTML, how it is used in web pages and the basics of the language.

Separating Form and Content:

Week 7 — Separating Form and Content

In this week we dipped into cascading style sheets and looked how they would be used alongside HTML to style a web page. We also looked at some redundant ways of styling within HTML and how we should not use these methods or techniques.
